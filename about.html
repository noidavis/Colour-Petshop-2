About Colour Petshop

Colour Petshop is an online database of animals that need homes. Pet lovers can search for pet that best matches their needs from the comfort of their personal computers. Colour Petshop also provides articles and materials that will help new and previews pet adoptors.

Our Mission

To use the power of Internet to:

1. Increase public awareness of the availability of high-quality adoptable pets

2. Increase the effectiveness of adoption program in the Philippines to the extent that the euthanasia of adoptable pets is eliminated

3. Elevate the status of pets to thaat of a family member
